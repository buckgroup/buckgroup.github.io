CAPT Nick Buck, USN (Retired)

President & Chief Executive Officer


After completing a twenty-six year Navy career where he served as a major defense acquisition program manager, Source Selection Authority and Fee Determining Official, Nick Buck established BCG in order to "help the government get better capability...faster." Since August 2010, BCG has provided superior strategic advisory services to companies doing business in the federal space. The BCG team translates mission needs and matches client solutions, in order to address challenging requirements in the DoD and Intelligence Community. Getting to mutual understanding of the government problem sooner allows clients to identify effective solutions faster, which reduces procurement time and boosts sales productivity. Bottom line: if we can solve the problem in 5 meetings instead of 10 we have saved time, energy, and money. (More →)
